About

Try Everything And No-one Fails (TEANF) is an ongoing archive documenting recordings, broadcasts, publications, films, photography, performances, and related creative materials produced between 2021 and the present.

Established as a framework for independent releases and collaborative projects, the archive has expanded into a growing collection of recorded works, source materials, publications, and historical artifacts.
